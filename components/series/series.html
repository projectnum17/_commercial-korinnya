One of the main areas of Korinya's work is research and dissemination ofUkrainian documentary photography.

With the help of our team, you will be able to familiarize yourself with the collection of unique series and projects of Ukrainian documentary photographers. Our main goal is to show the diversity of our country through the lenses of Ukrainian photographers. In our collection, you will find photographs that capture a variety of subjects, from unforgettable moments from the lives of rural residents to images of megacities and their inhabitants. Our photographers show our country from new angles, revealing hidden sides and unusual details of everyday life.
