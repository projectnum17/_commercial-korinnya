Korinnia -

is a documentary and reflexive community that aims to unite Ukrainian photographers to develop and popularize Ukrainian documentary photography.

Our team consists of young photographers who jointly explore the history of Ukrainian photography.  The community's activities include the creation of cultural projects, events, and exhibitions.

It is important for us to give the opportunity to express, study, and broadcast the views of powerful authors for the development of the visual culture of Ukraine. The starting point of our community is to try to open new perspectives for the younger generation and inspire them to create and reflect.

Hello! 
The Korinnia platform was created to distribute and broadcast materials about contemporary Ukrainian documentary photography and is a powerful tool for researching the processes of contemporary visual culture. 
We are happy to share our research and provide access to our materials, so we ask you to use the rules of reprinting when using our content, namely: 
1. Reprints must be up-to-date and completely truthful and reliable.
2. Reprints should begin with an author's link to the original article.
3. The reprint should reflect the identity of the original text.
4. The reprint must be checked for compliance with copyright and third-party copyrights.
5. The reprint must be fully posted on your website or blog.
6. You must put a banner or text link back to the original site or article.
7. Reprints should not be used for profit. It is forbidden to use the material for commercial purposes without the author's permission.
8. It is not forbidden to change the format and content of the material, but the material should not be subjectively distorted.
